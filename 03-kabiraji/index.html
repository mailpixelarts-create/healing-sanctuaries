The Lineage

Sensei Haruki Tanaka

Sensei Tanaka descended from a lineage of Kabiraji practitioners spanning seven generations in Kyoto. The art of Kabiraji involves drawing energy patterns above the body to extract and transmute stagnant chi.

Unlike Reiki, Kabiraji works with visible energy drawing — the practitioner's hands create intricate patterns that are felt as heat, vibration, and release.

Sacred Art

Three Pillars of Kabiraji

一

Energy Extraction Drawing

Hand movements that draw stagnation from the body's energy field. The practitioner's hands become conduits, pulling tangled energy into coherent patterns for release.

二

Five Point Harmonization

Crystal placement on the five major dantians — energy centers that govern the flow of life force through the physical and subtle bodies.

三

Ki Flow Restoration

Dynamic movement meditation to restore life force circulation. The body remembers its natural rhythm when guided by ancient patterns of movement.
