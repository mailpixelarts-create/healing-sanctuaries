# 03 — Yoga: Organic Editorial
## Image Prompts

### Hero Image — Chakra Wheel
A top-down photograph of a person in a cross-legged meditation pose, shot from directly above. Around their body, seven glowing orbs of light are arranged in a vertical line from the base of the spine to the crown of the head — red, orange, yellow, green, blue, indigo, violet. Each orb pulses softly, casting its color onto the dark floor beneath. The background is warm dark brown (#1A1612). The person wears simple white cotton clothing. The image is symmetrical, sacred, and organic. The chakra colors are vivid but not neon — they feel natural, like colored glass catching sunlight. 1:1 aspect ratio.

### About Section — Elena Portrait
A portrait of a woman in her mid-30s, seated in a yoga studio with natural light streaming through large windows. She is not in a pose — she is sitting casually, one leg tucked under her, hands resting on her knee. She wears simple, earth-toned athletic wear. Her expression is calm, present, unhurried. Behind her, the studio is warm wood and natural light. Plants on the windowsill. A stack of yoga blocks in the corner. She looks like someone who has made peace with her body. Medium shot, natural light, 85mm. 3:2 aspect ratio.

### Mechanism Section — The Breath-Body Connection
A scientific illustration showing the connection between breath and the nervous system. On the left: a pair of lungs inhaling, rendered in warm tones. On the right: a nervous system diagram, with the vagus nerve highlighted in gold, running from the brainstem down through the chest and abdomen. A dotted line connects the diaphragm to the vagus nerve, showing the mechanical link. The background is warm cream (#F5EDE3). Clean, educational, but not clinical. The style is editorial illustration, not medical textbook. 16:9 aspect ratio.

### Services Section — Three Cards
Three vertical panels showing yoga practices:
1. **Hip Opener**: A person in a deep pigeon pose, shot from the side. Warm natural light. The body is relaxed, not straining. The pose looks like surrender, not effort.
2. **Shoulder Release**: A person standing, arms wrapped around themselves in a self-hug, head bowed. The pose suggests release, not performance. Warm tones.
3. **Spinal Twist**: A person seated, twisting gently to one side. The spine is long, the twist is subtle. The image emphasizes length, not rotation.
All three: warm natural light, earth tones, minimal background. 3:1 triptych.

### Process Section — Three Steps
Three images showing the arc of a Slow Flow class:
1. **Dawn Arrival**: A person stepping onto a yoga mat in a dim studio, the first light of dawn visible through a window. The room is still, quiet, waiting. Wide shot.
2. **Deep Hold**: Close-up of a person's hands pressing into the mat during a pose. The fingers are spread, the knuckles are white with gentle pressure. The detail is intimate, focused.
3. **Savasana**: The person lying flat on their back, arms at their sides, eyes closed. The room is now fully lit by morning sun. The light falls across their body in warm stripes. Peaceful, complete.
Warm, organic, natural. 1:1 per image.

### Testimonial Section
An empty yoga studio at dawn. A single mat is unrolled in the center of the room. Morning light streams through floor-to-ceiling windows, casting long golden shadows across the wooden floor. A block and strap rest beside the mat. The room smells of wood and sweat and quiet. No people. The space holds the memory of practice. 16:9 aspect ratio.

### Footer Background
A seamless texture of woven organic cotton in warm cream and brown tones. The weave is visible, tactile, handmade. The texture suggests natural materials, earth, grounding. Warm palette: cream, tan, soft brown. 2:1 aspect ratio.

### OG / Social Share Image
Seven colored orbs arranged in a vertical line against a dark background (#1A1612). From bottom to top: red, orange, yellow, green, blue, indigo, violet. Each orb glows softly, casting a faint halo of its color. The orbs are perfectly spaced, perfectly round, perfectly balanced. The image is minimal, sacred, and iconic. 1.91:1 aspect ratio (1200x630px).
