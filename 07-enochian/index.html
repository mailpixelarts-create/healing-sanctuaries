The Enochian Language of the Angels

Oracle Cassandra works with the Enochian language received by Dr. John Dee and Edward Kelley in the 16th century. Enochian healing involves communicating with angelic beings through the Enochian tablets to receive healing frequencies.

Each of the 30 Aethyrs corresponds to a different aspect of consciousness and healing. The Enochian system provides a precise language for communicating with higher dimensional beings who offer specific healing energies for body, mind, and spirit.

Her Glastonbury sanctuary, located in the heart of England's most mystical landscape, serves as a gateway between earthly and angelic realms. The sacred geometry of the Enochian system, combined with the powerful ley line energy of Glastonbury, creates a uniquely potent healing environment.
