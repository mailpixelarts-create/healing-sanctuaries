03 — Philosophy

Foundations of Balance

I

The Three Doshas

Vata (air + ether), Pitta (fire + water), and Kapha (earth + water) are not merely body types — they are the fundamental forces governing all biological processes. Every person carries all three; health is their equilibrium.

II

Prakriti Is Intelligence

Your birth constitution (Prakriti) is your blueprint for optimal living. Ayurveda does not treat disease — it restores alignment with your original nature.

III

Nature as Pharmacy

Every healing substance exists within the natural world. Ayurveda's materia medica encompasses thousands of plants, minerals, and formulations — each carrying specific energetic signatures.

IV

Rhythm Is Medicine

Circadian cycles, seasonal rhythms, and daily routines (Dinacharya) form the temporal framework of Ayurvedic health. Healing happens when we live in time with nature's patterns.
