Dr. Elijah Temporis

The Quantum Physicist Who Found the Space Between Seconds

Dr. Elijah Temporis began his career as a quantum physicist at CERN, where he spent fifteen years studying the nature of time at the subatomic level. His groundbreaking research led him to a startling discovery: emotional trauma exists outside of linear time.

Chronokinesis Healing works with the premise that past traumas and future anxieties exist as energetic imprints in the body's temporal field. These imprints are not memories — they are living, breathing distortions in the time fabric of the body, creating physical symptoms, emotional patterns, and spiritual blockages that traditional therapy cannot reach.

Through guided meditation and breathwork, clients can access and heal these time-imprints without altering the timeline itself. Dr. Temporis holds sessions in his Greenwich sanctuary, a converted observatory overlooking the Prime Meridian — the very line from which the world measures time.

Healing Modalities

01

Temporal Field Scan

Identifying energetic imprints from past and future traumas in the body's time field. Guided journey to harmonize fragmented time-imprints. In this deep meditative state, the client revisits the temporal coordinates where trauma imprints reside. By consciously breathing at these scales, the client expands their awareness beyond linear time, allowing temporal imprints to dissolve naturally.
