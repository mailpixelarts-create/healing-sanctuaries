Chariot of Light, Vehicle of the Soul

Teacher Seraphina channels teachings from the Ascended Masters on Merkaba activation. The Merkaba — meaning "chariot" in Hebrew — is a star tetrahedron of light that surrounds every human body. It is the light body, the vehicle through which consciousness travels between dimensions of existence.

In most people, the Merkaba is dormant — a potential energy field that has never been activated. Through specific breathing patterns, sacred geometry meditation, and conscious intention, the Merkaba can be set into motion, spinning at rates that allow the practitioner to access higher states of consciousness, heal at the cellular level, and ultimately achieve spiritual ascension.

Teacher Seraphina's sanctuary sits at the foot of Mount Shasta, one of the most powerful spiritual vortexes on Earth. The mountain's crystalline energy amplifies Merkaba activation work exponentially. Her teachings draw from the Emerald Tablets of Thoth, the Merkaba literature of Drunvalo Melchizedek, and the direct transmissions she receives from the Ascended Masters.

Healing Modalities

01

Merkaba Breathing

The 18-breath activation sequence to spin the star tetrahedron. This precise breathing pattern, performed in three sets of six breaths, activates the counter-rotating fields of the Merkaba. Each breath corresponds to a specific geometric rotation, gradually building the energy field until it reaches the speed of light and becomes a vehicle for consciousness.

02

Sacred Geometry Meditation

Visualizing the complex geometry of the Merkaba field. The practitioner guides the client through the intricate forms of the star tetrahedron — two interlocking pyramids, one pointing up and one down, creating a three-dimensional Star of David. This meditation strengthens the client's ability to hold and manipulate sacred geometric forms in consciousness.

03

Light Body Integration

Grounding expanded consciousness into physical reality. After Merkaba activation, the body must adjust to the higher frequencies of the light body. This integration session helps the client embody their expanded awareness, ensuring that spiritual insights become lived reality rather than fleeting experiences.
