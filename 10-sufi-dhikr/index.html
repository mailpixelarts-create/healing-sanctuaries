About Chakra Balancing

Sheikh Rashid al-Qadiri

Fez, Morocco

Sheikh Rashid al-Qadiri is a renowned energy healer who bridges West African spiritual traditions with the ancient Indian chakra system. His unique approach combines ancestral wisdom with sacred geometry to restore balance to the body's seven energy centers.

Based in Fez, Morocco, Rashid has spent over fifteen years studying the intersection of Sufi dhikr practices and Vedic energy work. He believes that true healing occurs when we align our energy centers — not just physically, but spiritually and culturally.

His sanctuary at 777 Energy Circle is a space where ancient wisdom meets modern understanding, where the geometry of the universe meets the geometry of the soul. Each session is a journey through the seven gates of transformation.

The Science

The Mechanism

Chakras are neuro-endocrine junctions — plexuses where nerve clusters meet hormone-secreting glands. The Root chakra corresponds to the adrenal medulla. The Crown corresponds to the pineal gland. Balancing is not metaphor. It is hormone-level modulation.

When we work with the chakra system, we are working with the body's own communication network. Each center governs specific hormonal outputs that influence mood, energy, sleep, and emotional regulation. Blockage at any point creates downstream effects — not just energetically, but biochemically.

Our Philosophy

Four pillars of chakra wisdom

01

The Body Remembers

Every chakra stores not just energy, but memory. The root holds ancestral fear, the sacral carries creative joy, the solar plexus guards personal power. Healing means listening to what the body has been holding, and gently releasing what no longer serves.

02

Geometry is Language

Sacred geometry — the flower of life, Metatron's cube, the Sri Yantra — is the blueprint of creation. Each chakra corresponds to specific geometric patterns. When we align with these patterns, we align with the fundamental structure of the universe itself.

03

Color is Frequency

Each energy center vibrates at a specific frequency, visible as color. Red at the base, violet at the crown. When a chakra is blocked, its color dims. Through color therapy and intention, we restore the full spectrum of light within the body.

04

Balance is Dynamic

True balance is not static — it is a continuous dance. Like the spinning wheel, the chakras must remain in motion. Our practice teaches not to fixate on any single center, but to cultivate the fluid movement of energy through all seven gates simultaneously.

Questions

Frequently Asked

"Are there really 7 chakras?"
We use 7. There are over 100. The seven we work with are the primary junctions — the major crossroads where energy converges most densely. The others are satellite centers, refinements, subtleties. Master the seven, and the rest reveal themselves.
"Why do I feel dizzy during a session?"
The pineal gland is recalibrating. It sits at the center of the brain, governing circadian rhythm and melatonin production. When energy moves through the Crown, the pineal responds. Dizziness is temporary. Hydrate. Breathe. It passes.
"Can I do this myself?"
Yes, with audio-guided visualizations. You do not need a practitioner to access your own energy. A practitioner offers perspective — a mirror, not a map. The map is already inside you.
"How many sessions before I notice a change?"
Most people feel a shift after the first session — a lightness, a clarity, an emotional release. But deep, lasting change typically requires 3 to 6 sessions, depending on how long the blockages have been present. The body has its own timeline. We respect it.
"Is chakra balancing a religion?"
No. We use no deities, no dogma, no belief system required. The chakra system is an energetic framework — like acupuncture meridians or the nervous system. It exists whether you believe in it or not. The body does not require your faith to respond to touch.
"What if I cry or have an emotional release?"
This is one of the most common and most welcome responses. Tears, laughter, trembling, heat — these are signs that energy is moving through stored emotional tissue. The body holds what the mind has pushed away. When the energy flows, the stored material surfaces. Let it pass. Do not hold on. Do not push away. Simply witness.
