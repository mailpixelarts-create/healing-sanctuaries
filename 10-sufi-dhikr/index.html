About Chakra Balancing

Nakia chakra-Wellness is a renowned energy healer who bridges West African spiritual traditions with the ancient Indian chakra system. Her unique approach combines ancestral wisdom with sacred geometry to restore balance to the body's seven energy centers.

Based in Accra, Ghana, Nakia has spent over fifteen years studying the intersection of African spiritual practices and Vedic energy work. She believes that true healing occurs when we align our energy centers — not just physically, but spiritually and culturally.

Her sanctuary at 777 Energy Circle is a space where ancient wisdom meets modern understanding, where the geometry of the universe meets the geometry of the soul. Each session is a journey through the seven gates of transformation.

Our Philosophy

Four pillars of chakra wisdom

01

The Body Remembers

Every chakra stores not just energy, but memory. The root holds ancestral fear, the sacral carries creative joy, the solar plexus guards personal power. Healing means listening to what the body has been holding, and gently releasing what no longer serves.

02

Geometry is Language

Sacred geometry — the flower of life, Metatron's cube, the Sri Yantra — is the blueprint of creation. Each chakra corresponds to specific geometric patterns. When we align with these patterns, we align with the fundamental structure of the universe itself.

03

Color is Frequency

Each energy center vibrates at a specific frequency, visible as color. Red at the base, violet at the crown. When a chakra is blocked, its color dims. Through color therapy and intention, we restore the full spectrum of light within the body.

04

Balance is Dynamic

True balance is not static — it is a continuous dance. Like the spinning wheel, the chakras must remain in motion. Our practice teaches not to fixate on any single center, but to cultivate the fluid movement of energy through all seven gates simultaneously.

Your First Visit

The Journey

What to expect when you arrive

Arrival & Grounding

Arrive 15 minutes early. You'll be welcomed into a space of warm light and sacred geometry. Take a moment to breathe, to ground yourself, to leave the outside world at the door. Tea is offered. Silence is honored.

Energy Assessment

Nakia will guide you through a gentle assessment of your seven energy centers. Through conversation, intuition, and proximity reading, she identifies which chakras are overactive, underactive, or blocked. This forms the map for your session.

The Balancing

Using a combination of meditation, color therapy, sound healing, and sacred geometry, Nakia works with each energy center. You may feel warmth, tingling, emotional release, or profound stillness. Every experience is valid. Every sensation is information.

Integration & Guidance

After the session, you'll receive personalized guidance for maintaining your energetic balance — including specific affirmations, color practices, and sound frequencies to work with at home. The healing continues long after you leave.

What to Wear Comfortable, loose clothing in white or light colors. The body needs to be free to receive energy.
What to Bring An open heart and willingness to experience. All crystals, bowls, and sacred tools are provided.
Who is Welcome All people, regardless of background or belief. Energy knows no boundaries. Neither do we.
