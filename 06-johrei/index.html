About the Healer

Master Aiko Takahashi

Master Aiko Takahashi Portrait

Direct Student of Mokichi Okada's Teachings

Master Aiko is a direct student of the teachings of Mokichi Okada, founder of Johrei. The practice involves channeling divine light through the palms to purify the receiver's spirit and physical body.

Johrei does not heal directly — it purifies the spirit, and the purified spirit then heals the body naturally. This fundamental principle distinguishes Johrei from other energy healing modalities.

Her Kyoto sanctuary, nestled among ancient temples and zen gardens, provides the ideal environment for this purification practice. The golden light of Johrei, when channeled with pure intention, creates a field of healing that transcends physical touch and speaks directly to the soul's capacity for self-healing.

The Sacred Art

Healing Modalities

01

Palm Channeling

Directing Johrei light through the healer's palms. The practitioner positions their hands to channel divine universal light, which flows through the palms and into the recipient's body. This light seeks out spiritual impurities and dissolves them, allowing the body's natural healing processes to activate.

02

Spirit Purification Meditation

Seated practice to increase light channeling capacity. Through dedicated meditation and spiritual cultivation, the practitioner develops their ability to channel Johrei light. This practice involves specific breathing techniques, visualization, and alignment with universal consciousness.

03

Group Johrei Circle

Multiple practitioners creating a healing field. When several Johrei practitioners gather with shared intention, the healing field they create amplifies exponentially. The collective channeling of divine light creates a transformative space where deep spiritual purification occurs.
