The Healer

Master Aiko Takahashi

Direct student of the teachings of Mokichi Okada, founder of Johrei. A practice of channeling divine light through the palms to purify the receiver's spirit and physical body.

Johrei does not heal directly — it purifies the spirit, and the purified spirit then heals the body naturally.

Sacred Art

Palm Channeling

Directing Johrei light through the healer's palms. A transmission of divine energy that dissolves impurities in the spiritual body.

Spirit Purification Meditation

Seated practice to increase light channeling capacity. Stillness becomes the vessel. The practitioner opens, the light enters.

Group Johrei Circle

Multiple practitioners creating a healing field. Shared intention amplifies the light. A circle of purity without words.
