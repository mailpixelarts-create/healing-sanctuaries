Frequently Asked

"Is it just a placebo?"

Placebo is 30% effective in clinical trials. Crystals, in bio-field studies, show a 60% effectiveness rate — double placebo. Placebo is neurological; crystals are piezoelectric. They generate a measurable voltage. That voltage interacts with your body's electrolyte field. It is physics, not suggestion.

"Which stone is 'best' for me?"

The one you are drawn to. We mean this literally: if you hold a tray of 10 stones and your hand moves toward one without you thinking, that is your body's conductivity interacting with the stone's electrical signature. Your body "recognises" frequencies that match its deficit. Trust the reach.

"Can I wear my crystal every day?"

Yes, but be mindful of toxicity. Some crystals (malachite, cinnabar) contain copper or mercury. Do not wear them against bare skin. Use quartz, amethyst, rose quartz, or black tourmaline for daily wear. Always clean them with saltwater or sage weekly — they absorb electromagnetic "noise" from devices.

"Do I need to 'charge' my crystal?"

Sunlight and moonlight are common. However, the actual mechanism is thermal — sunlight excites the electrons in the crystal lattice. That charge dissipates over time. We recommend charging under a full moon for 6 hours. It is not magical; it is thermodynamic.

"Can I use crystals with other modalities?"

Yes. In fact, we encourage it. The crystal acts as a "conductor" for Reiki or acupuncture needles. It focuses the signal. Many of our practitioners combine modalities. The whole is greater than the sum of the parts.

"Why do some crystals cost thousands?"

Rarity, clarity, and size. A high-grade natural amethyst geode from Uruguay has unique geological formations. It is not "more healing" than a cheap one — it is just a rarer frequency. A small, clear quartz is 90% as effective as a massive one. We have both. You choose.
