The Healer

Master Aiko Takahashi

Direct student of the teachings of Mokichi Okada, founder of Johrei. A practice of channeling divine light through the palms to purify the receiver's spirit and physical body.

For over thirty years, Master Takahashi has devoted her life to the study and transmission of Johrei. She trained under Master Masahiro Tanaka in Kyoto, where she spent twelve years in silent practice before beginning her own teaching lineage.

Her approach is rooted in the belief that the human spirit is inherently luminous — that illness and suffering are not punishments but accumulations of spiritual impurity that can be gently dissolved through the channeling of divine light.

She has offered Johrei to thousands of individuals across Japan, Southeast Asia, and North America. Her sanctuary in Kyoto remains open to all who seek purification without condition.

Sacred Art

Palm Channeling

Directing Johrei light through the healer's palms. A transmission of divine energy that dissolves impurities in the spiritual body. The receiver may feel warmth, tingling, or a deep sense of peace — as though a long-held weight has been gently lifted.

The healer raises one hand toward the crown of the head, the other toward the chest. Light flows between the palms in a continuous current. Sessions typically last twenty to forty minutes, though time becomes irrelevant in the presence of the light.

Spirit Purification Meditation

Seated practice to increase light channeling capacity. Stillness becomes the vessel. The practitioner opens, the light enters. This is the foundation of all Johrei work — the daily discipline that keeps the channel clear.

Performed at dawn and dusk, the meditation involves specific breathing patterns and visualization of light entering through the crown chakra. Over months and years, the practitioner's capacity to channel light increases exponentially. The body becomes a living conduit.

Group Johrei Circle

Multiple practitioners creating a healing field. Shared intention amplifies the light. A circle of purity without words. The field generated by five or more practitioners can be felt across an entire room.

Held on the full moon of each month, the circle gathers in silence. Practitioners stand in a ring, palms raised, channeling light inward toward a central point. The accumulated energy creates a vortex of purification that benefits all participants and extends outward to the community.

Philosophy

The Light of Johrei

The Spirit Is Primary

All illness begins in the spiritual body before manifesting in the physical. Johrei addresses the root, not the symptom. When the spirit is purified, the body follows its natural design — wholeness.

Light Is Not Earned

Divine light flows to all beings equally. It does not choose the worthy. Johrei is not a reward — it is a birthright. The healer does not decide who receives; the healer simply channels.

Purification, Not Cure

Johrei does not "fix" the body. It dissolves the impurities that prevent the body from healing itself. The distinction is everything. We do not impose health — we remove the obstacles to it.

The Healer Is a Vessel

The practitioner is not the source of the light. They are the channel through which it flows. Humility is the foundation of Johrei — the moment the ego enters, the light dims.

Gratitude Heals

Mokichi Okada taught that gratitude is the highest vibration of the human spirit. A grateful heart is an open channel. In the presence of gratitude, impurities dissolve naturally — even without formal Johrei.

Journey

Your First Visit

01

Arrival & Silence

Enter the sanctuary and remove your shoes at the threshold. A simple tea will be offered. There is no need to speak. Allow the silence to settle around you like a garment.

02

Intention Setting

Master Takahashi will ask a single question: "What have you come to release?" Answer honestly, or do not answer at all. The light knows what the mouth cannot say.

03

The Channeling

You will be seated or lying down. The healer stands before you, palms raised. Light is transmitted. You may feel warmth, tingling, emotional release, or nothing at all. All responses are valid. All responses are the light working.

04

Integration

After the channeling, remain still for as long as you need. Water is available. The healer will not rush you. Some find clarity immediately; others carry the light with them for days before its meaning becomes clear.
