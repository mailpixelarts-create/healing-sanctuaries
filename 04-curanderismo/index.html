Philosophy

Present Moment Awareness

The past is a memory, the future is a projection. Only the now is real. We train the mind to rest here, in the immediacy of experience.

Non-Attachment

Observing thoughts and emotions without clinging or resistance. They arise, they pass. We are the sky, not the clouds.

Compassionate Inquiry

Looking deeply into the nature of suffering with kindness. Understanding transforms. The wound becomes the door to wisdom.

Embodied Stillness

Stillness is not the absence of movement but its deepest expression. In the body, we find the ground of being.

Interbeing

We are not separate. The breath connects us to all beings. Meditation reveals the profound interdependence of all life.
