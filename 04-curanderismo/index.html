Carrying the Medicine Wheel of Her Ancestors

Maestra Rosa carries the medicine wheel of her ancestors, blending Aztec, Mayan, and Spanish Catholic healing traditions into a comprehensive system of spiritual medicine.

Curanderismo treats the whole person — body, mind, and soul — through limpias (spiritual cleansings), herbal remedies, and ceremony. She specializes in empacho (digestive blockages), susto (soul loss), and mal de ojo (evil eye).

Her San Francisco sanctuary in the Mission District serves as a bridge between ancient Mesoamerican healing wisdom and the needs of modern communities. Each ceremony honors the traditions of her lineage while addressing the unique spiritual needs of every individual who enters her healing space.

The Sacred Art

Healing Modalities

01

Egg Limpia

Sacred egg rolled over the body to absorb negative energy. The egg serves as a spiritual diagnostic tool and energy sponge, drawing out stagnation, negative attachments, and spiritual impurities that have accumulated in the body's energy field.

02

Herbal Bath Ceremony

Botanical preparations for spiritual purification using traditional healing plants: rue for protection, rosemary for cleansing, chamomile for peace, and marigold for connecting with ancestral spirits. Each bath is prepared according to the specific needs of the individual.

03

Soul Retrieval Ceremony

Guided journey to recover fragmented soul parts lost through trauma, shock, or grief. Through prayer, drumming, and spiritual invocation, the practitioner calls back the lost essence and restores it to the individual, completing the healing of deep spiritual wounds.
