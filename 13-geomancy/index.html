Thirty Years Walking the Ley Lines

Earth Keeper Rowan has spent thirty years mapping and working with the earth's energy lines, sacred sites, and power places. His journey began at Avebury, where he first felt the earth's energy pulse beneath his feet — and knew that his life's work was to listen to what the land had to say.

Geomancy is the art of reading and harmonizing the energy of the land itself. Illness, both personal and planetary, arises when the relationship between humans and the earth is disrupted. When we build on sacred sites without permission, divert waterways, or poison the soil, we sever the ancient covenant that sustains all life.

Rowan's work restores this covenant. Using divining rods, crystal pendulums, and direct earth-sensing abilities developed over decades, he reads the energy patterns of the land and works to restore harmony. His Glastonbury sanctuary sits on a convergence of three major ley lines, making it one of the most powerful earth energy healing centers in Britain.

The Sacred Art

Healing Modalities

01

Ley Line Alignment

Tuning personal energy to the earth's sacred geometry network. Ley lines are invisible channels of earth energy that connect sacred sites across the landscape. By aligning one's personal energy field with these lines, the client receives the healing vibrations that the earth naturally transmits through its meridian system.

02

Site Clearing Ceremony

Releasing stagnant or negative energy from land and buildings. When trauma occurs on a piece of land — battlefields, accident sites, places of suffering — the energy becomes trapped in the earth. This ceremony releases that trapped energy and restores the land to its natural state of purity and vitality.

03

Earth Acupuncture

Placing crystals and sacred objects at key points to restore land harmony. Just as acupuncture needles balance the body's energy meridians, Earth Acupuncture balances the earth's energy meridians. Rowan places crystals, stones, and sacred objects at precise locations to restore flow and harmony to disrupted land energy.
