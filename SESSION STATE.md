## Key Doctrine References

- **Section XXI (Motion Doctrine):** Motion exists to communicate. Never animate by habit.
- **Section XXIV (Signature Interaction):** ONE memorable interaction per site. Not many.
- **Section XX (Scroll Choreography):** Reveal → Pause → Accelerate → Breathe → Seduce → Expand → Reflect
- **Section XXIII (AJAX Experience):** Navigation should never feel disruptive.
- **Section XXVII (Footer Worldbuilding):** Footers conclude experiences. Emotional endings.
- **Section XIV (Anti-Generic Guard):** Reject template thinking. No section stacking without narrative.
- **Section VII (North Star):** Would this make someone stop scrolling, hold their breath, and remember this experience tomorrow?
