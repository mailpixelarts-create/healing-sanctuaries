Master Wei Linzhao Portrait

22 Years of Taoist Training at Wudang Mountain

Master Wei trained at Wudang Mountain under Taoist masters for 22 years, immersing himself in the ancient traditions of internal cultivation and energetic medicine that have been practiced for thousands of years.

Daode Xin Xi translates as "Way-Virtue Heart-Matter" — a system of energetic cultivation that works with the three dantians, the microcosmic orbit, and the five element cycle to restore harmony between heaven, earth, and human.

Master Wei's approach bridges ancient Taoist wisdom with contemporary understanding of energy medicine. His Berkeley sanctuary offers a space where the principles of wu wei (non-forced action) guide every healing interaction, allowing the body's natural qi to find its own path to balance.

The Sacred Art

Healing Modalities

01

Microcosmic Orbit Meditation

Circulation of qi through the governing and conception vessels, creating a continuous flow of vital energy that nourishes every organ and tissue. This foundational practice opens the body's primary energy pathways and cultivates deep inner stillness.

02

Five Element Balancing

Seasonal and organ-based harmonization aligned with the Taoist five element cycle: wood, fire, earth, metal, and water. Each element corresponds to specific organs, emotions, and seasons, and balancing them restores the body's natural rhythm.

03

Inner Smile Practice

Seated meditation directing healing energy to each organ through the cultivation of genuine inner joy. The practitioner smiles inwardly, sending warmth and positive energy to the heart, liver, lungs, kidneys, and spleen, awakening each organ's healing capacity.
