### Hero Image — Waveform Visualizer

Sound waves are visible as concentric ripples emanating from the bowl, rendered as luminous blue (#4A90D9) oscilloscope lines that fill the air like the rings of Saturn. The floor is reflective black, mirroring the waves. The background is deep ocean blue-black (#060A10). Particles of light drift upward like bioluminescent plankton. The mood is immersive, hypnotic, and vast. Shot on anamorphic lens, 2.39:1 aspect ratio.

1. **Crystal Singing Bowl**: A frosted white crystal bowl on a dark surface, glowing faintly from within. Blue light refracts through the crystal. Close-up, shallow DOF.
2. **Gong**: A large bronze gong suspended in a dark room. A mallet strikes the center, sending visible blue ripples through the air. Dramatic side-lighting.

### Process Section — Three Steps
Three images showing the progression of a sound healing session:
1. **Preparation**: A person lying on a mat in a candlelit room, surrounded by crystal bowls and gongs. Wide shot, atmospheric, warm-cool contrast.
2. **Immersion**: Close-up of the person's chest rising and falling, with visible blue sound waves passing through their body. The waves are rendered as translucent light. Intimate, powerful.
3. **Stillness**: The same person in the same position, but now the waves have settled. The room is quieter. A faint blue glow lingers on their skin. Peaceful, resolved.
Cinematic, dark, immersive. 1:1 per image.
